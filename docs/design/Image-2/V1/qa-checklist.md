# Image2 QA Checklist

## Structure

- Preserve the original page type and major layout skeleton.
- Keep sidebar, main content start line, top role, CTA hierarchy, section frame, and timeline / record rhythm recognizable.
- Do not collapse long clinical pages into generic dashboard cards.

## Theme Unity

- Dark and light must feel like two modes of one designed object.
- Dark uses black clinical command surfaces, thin luminous rules, high-contrast type, and sharp orange accent.
- Light uses forensic paper, ink rules, stamped labels, document grids, and clinical forms.
- The theme switch changes material and tone, not product identity or workflow.

## Chinese Legibility

- Chinese labels must remain legible and first-class.
- No pseudo-Chinese, no broken glyphs, no decorative baked-in text treatment.
- Do not use wide letter spacing on Chinese text.
- Dates, drug names, biomarkers, and abbreviations must remain readable and visually separated.

## Failure Conditions

Regenerate if any output shows:

- Generic SaaS dashboard style.
- Soft pastel UI, blue enterprise UI, purple gradients, decorative blobs, or stock medical imagery.
- Rounded card-heavy layout that ignores Firefly-Isle's hard-edge editorial identity.
- Major page responsibilities moved or deleted.
- Light and dark looking like separate products.

## Component Strip Requirements

The component strip must show:

- Sidebar item.
- Primary extraction button.
- Secondary export button.
- Textarea.
- Status chip.
- Timeline section.
- Patient metric.
- Missing-field warning.
- Verification stamp.

The strip is a design board, not a runtime page.
