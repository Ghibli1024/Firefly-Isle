# Design System Overview

## Theme: Fidelity

This design system is built upon a 'Fidelity' theme, emphasizing a sophisticated and classic aesthetic with a focus on clear information hierarchy and timeless appeal.

## Color Palette

The primary color mode for the UI is **light**.

*   **Primary Color:** `#111111` - A strong, deep black, providing a bold foundation for key interactive elements and branding.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#E5E5E0` - A very light, almost off-white grey, used for supporting elements, subtle borders, and background accents.
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#CC0000` - A vibrant, classic red, serving as an accent for highlights, alerts, or specific calls to attention.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#F9F9F7` - An extremely light, almost pure white, serving as the primary background and surface color, ensuring readability and spaciousness.

## Typography

The chosen typefaces contribute to a refined and editorial feel.

*   **Headlines:** `Newsreader` - A serif font chosen for its elegant and authoritative presence, ideal for conveying gravitas in titles and headings.
*   **Body Text:** `Noto Serif` - A versatile and highly readable serif font, ensuring comfortable reading for longer passages of text.
*   **Labels:** `Inter` - A clean and modern sans-serif font, providing clarity and functionality for interactive labels, buttons, and system text.

## Roundedness

UI elements feature **sharp, angular** corners (roundedness: 0), contributing to a precise and architectural aesthetic.

## Spacing

The design employs a **normal** level of whitespace (spacing: 2), ensuring a balanced and comfortable visual experience without feeling overly dense or sparse. This provides good readability and clear separation of elements.
